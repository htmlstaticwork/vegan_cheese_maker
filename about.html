Our Story

Redefining the art of cheesemaking. No dairy, no compromises. Just pure, cultured excellence.

Traditional techniques | Modern plant-based microbes

"Our journey began with a mission to preserve the heritage of artisanal aging while embracing the future of sustainable, dairy-free living."

The Genesis of Cheevo

It started with a simple question: Why should transitioning to a plant-based lifestyle mean the end of the sophisticated cheese board? Our founders, lifelong gastronomes, refused to accept the waxy, unfermented alternatives on the market.

Traveling to the historic caves of Roquefort and the lush pastures of Normandy, we studied under master affineurs. We learned that the magic of cheese isn't in the milkâ€”it's in the microbes, the time, and the environment.

By applying these centuries-old techniques to organic, ethically sourced nuts, Cheevo was born in a small commercial kitchen. Today, our climate-controlled cellars house thousands of aging wheels, each receiving the same love and attention as a traditional grand cru.

Our Mission

"To elevate plant-based cuisine through uncompromising craftsmanship, while honoring our commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing."

The Affinage Process

1. Sourcing

We use only Fair Trade, organic cashews, almonds, and macadamias as our base.

2. Inoculation

Proprietary vegan cultures and enzymes are introduced to start fermentation.

3. Aging

Wheels are aged in our humified caves from 3 weeks to 6 months.

4. Perfection

Each wheel is hand-turned, salted, and inspected before it reaches you.

5. Curation

Wheels are assessed daily by our masters to ensure peak maturity.

6. Delivery

Primal cooling and bespoke wrapping preserve the cheese's integrity.
